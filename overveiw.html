Trusted leaders in User experience


NN/g has an extensive legacy of thought leadership in design. The company was established in 1998 by two UX pioneers, Dr. Jakob Nielsen and Dr. Don Norman. Among their many contributions, Jakob developed the Ten Usability Heuristics and led the discount usability movement, while Don invented the term “user experience” and authored The Design of Everyday Things.

Digital Icons that Work: A Comprehensive Guide to Enhancing User Experience with Effective Iconography

Kate Kaplan and Kelley Gordan, 2024


Uncover a wealth of research-based guidelines for designing and leveraging icons in digital user interfaces. Based on primary research conducted by the author, this comprehensive guide provides in-depth insights into how users recognize, interpret, and assign meaning to digital iconography.

You will learn how to transform digital icons into powerful design elements that reinforce and clarify ideas and actions within user interfaces and even conduct your own research to inform icon-related design strategies.

Get Answers to These Questions


  • How do icons enhance and improve user experience?
  • Are there universally understood icons?
  • Which icons are best for representing common functions?
  • Which icons are widely recognized, and which may cause confusion?
  • What factors influence how users understand icons within user interfaces?
  • What makes a digital icon effective?
  • How can I apply research-based strategies to my icon design process?


Who should Read it


  • UX designers who want to use iconography confidently in digital interfaces
  • UX researchers who want to understand how to test and evaluate icons over time
  • Graphic designers who want guidance on creating clear and compelling digital icons
  • Marketing professionals who want to enhance user engagement
  • UI designers who want to build interfaces with effective iconography
  • Product managers who want to ensure that digital icons are optimized
  • Students or professionals interested in understanding how to use icons in digital products

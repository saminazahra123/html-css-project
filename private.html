Team Training & UX certification


Team training courses can help build a unified focus and an understanding of UX
approaches for your entire team.



Useful for All Types of Teams


Our private team training is ideal for teams who are:



  • Kicking off large, complex projects
  • Looking to change processes at scale
  • Expanding their UX knowledge and skills

How it work


Reach out to us to get started. We're happy to help you determine what will be best for your team.


  1. Choose a date when your team will be available.
  2. Choose one or more live UX courses. Most courses can be customized to meet your goals. Alternatively,
    we can design a custom training workshop for your team. Team training courses will be eligible for UX
    Certification     unless you choose to customize substantially.
  3. Choose virtual or in-person training. Check out the pricing below for details.
